FOOD CATEGORIES

Vegetarian

Vegetables are a good source of vitamins and minerals, including folate, vitamin C and potassium. Excellent source of dietary fibre, which can help to maintain a healthy gut. Reduce your risk of heart disease, stroke and some types of cancer.

View your veggie meals!
White Meat

Chicken and turkey are a good source of lean protein, vitamins and minerals.

View your white meat recipes!
Red Meat

Red meat provides us with iron, zinc and B vitamins. Meat is one of the main sources of vitamin B12 in the diet. Try to limit red meat to 70g a day.

View your red meat recipes!
Oily-Fish

A balanced diet should include at least 1 portion of oily fish a week. That's because fish and shellfish are good sources of many vitamins and minerals. Salmon and sardines are particularly high in long-chain omega-3 fatty acids, which help to keep your heart healthy.

View Oily-Fish recipes!
White Fish

Low in fat. some species can be a source of omega-3 fatty acids, e.g. sea bass, sea bream, turbot, halibut, but at lower levels than oily fish.
